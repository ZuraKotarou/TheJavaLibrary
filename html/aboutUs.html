ABOUT US

VISION

Our vision is for our website to be a main stop for readers looking for free, updated and quality articles on programming without spending money. To provide an educational blog where readers won't have to spend time navigating through complex pages just to find their articles.

MISSION

Our mission is to provide a straightforward, free and accessible articles on Java, Python and JavaScript programming languages. Readers can access our website, and find their articles without logging-in, paying, or navigating through complex pages.
